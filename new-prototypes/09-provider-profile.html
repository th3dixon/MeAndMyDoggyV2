About Sarah

I've been caring for pets professionally for over 5 years, and I absolutely love what I do! My passion for animals started early, and I've turned that love into a career helping pet parents give their furry friends the best care possible.

I offer a range of services including dog walking, pet sitting, and basic training. I'm experienced with dogs of all sizes and temperaments, from energetic puppies to senior dogs with special needs.

Your pet's safety and happiness are my top priorities. I'm fully insured, DBS checked, and have completed pet first aid training. I provide regular updates with photos and messages so you always know your pet is in good hands.
